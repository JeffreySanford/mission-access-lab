RBAC vs. ReBAC — what's actually different

Most teams reach for role-based access control (RBAC) by default: users get roles, roles carry permissions. OpenFGA implements relationship-based access control (ReBAC) instead — permissions are computed by walking a graph of relationships, not by looking up a role table.
